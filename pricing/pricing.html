Frequently Asked Questions

What is AutoApply?

AutoApply is a feature that automatically applies to tutoring jobs that match your specified criteria, saving you time and increasing your chances of finding opportunities.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your account settings. Changes will take effect immediately, and billing will be prorated.

Do you offer a free trial for Pro?

While we don't offer a separate free trial for the Pro plan, our Basic plan is completely free forever, allowing you to experience core features before committing.
